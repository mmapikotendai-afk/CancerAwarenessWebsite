Find hospitals and clinics for breast cancer screening and treatment

Finding Healthcare Services

Access to quality healthcare is essential for breast cancer screening, diagnosis, and treatment. Below is a comprehensive list of healthcare facilities across Zimbabwe's provinces where you can receive breast cancer services.

Important: It's recommended to call ahead to confirm available services, operating hours, and appointment requirements. Some facilities may require referrals or have specific screening schedules.

Important Information

Contact Before Visiting

Always call ahead to confirm operating hours, available services, and appointment requirements. Some facilities may require referrals from primary care providers.

üìã

What to Bring

Bring your identification, medical records if available, and insurance information. For screening appointments, wear comfortable clothing that's easy to remove.

üíä

Services Available

Many of these facilities offer breast cancer screening (mammograms, clinical exams), diagnosis, treatment, and follow-up care. Ask about specific services when calling.

üí∞

Costs & Insurance

Public hospitals typically have lower costs, while private facilities may offer shorter wait times. Check with your insurance provider about coverage for breast cancer screening and treatment.
